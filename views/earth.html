Welcome to Earth!

Here are some fun earth facts:

  • The earth is not a sphere. It is an oblate spherioid. It is mostly sphere shaped, but it is squished, and very bumpy.
  • The center of the earth is hot for several reasons, including radioactive elements decaying, and friction from elements moving within the earth's core due to gravity.
  • If you are reading this, you probably were born on the earth.
