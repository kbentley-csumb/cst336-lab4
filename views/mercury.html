Welcome to Mercury!

Here are some fun Mercury facts:

  • Mercury orbits the sun faster than any other planet.
  • Mercury can be seen during a total solar eclipse
  • The average temperature on Mercury is 332° Fahrenheit, but it gets as cold as -290° and as hot as 800°
  • Even though Mercury is the closest planet to the sun, it is not the hottest. The hottest planet in our solar system is Venus.
