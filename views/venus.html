Welcome to Venus!

Here are some fun Venus facts:

  • Venus has a very similar size to the planet Earth, and is sometimes referred to as its sister planet.
  • Venus is one of the brightest of all the planets and stars in the sky.
  • Venus has the hottest surface temperature of all the planets.
  • Gravity on Venus is about 91% of the earth's gravity. But it is covered by acid clouds, so you probably don't want to live there.
